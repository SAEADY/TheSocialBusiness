Disinformation Age

Social Media has been important for spreading important messages, like during the #BlackLivesMatter movement or the #MeToo movement. However, many argue that social media does not have enough regulations in place to prevent false news from spreading especially as it becomes more and more people's primary source for news.
Over 80% of Americans now get news from digital devices, surpassing TV, radio, and print. Among young adults, social media is the top news source. Platforms like Twitter, Facebook, and TikTok act as news hubs, blurring opinions and facts.

NewsGuard, a journalism and technology tool that tracks online information, analyzed 540 TikTok results , examining the top 20 from 27 searches on news topics. They found that nearly 2/10 videos contained false or misleading claims.
